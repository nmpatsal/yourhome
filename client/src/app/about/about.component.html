What is a Business Apartment?

A business apartment has a hall, kitchen, bathroom, living room and bedroom (one, two or three bedrooms).

Tastefully furnished, fully appointed kitchen, washing machine/tumble dryer, cable TV, radio, CD-player and telephone as well as internet connection

If you are interested in an apartment with a high service level where nothing is left to chance, the possibility is here.

A solid 24-hour service programme ensures repairs, maintenance, administration and comfort.
